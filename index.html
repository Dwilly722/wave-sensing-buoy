Wave Sensing Buoy

Mechatronics Project

About the Project

The Wave Sensing Buoy is a project fundamentally designed with safety in mind. Utilizing components such as the GAC mini IMU, Elegoo Uno, Four RGB LEDs, an LCD screen, and polyurethane expanding foam, our buoy is able to:

  • Establish a baseline wave-acceleration
  • Sense the presence of dangerous waves
  • Display color-coordinated warnings for low, medium, and high-strength waves
  • Store wave data over extended periods

These multifaceted functions allow it to act as both a safety device and a localized environmental research tool, promoting peace of mind and enabling data collection on the water.

Key Components

  • GAC Mini IMU
  • Elegoo Uno Microcontroller
  • 4 RGB LEDs
  • LCD Display Screen
  • Polyurethane Expanding Foam (for buoyancy)

Warning System

  • Green: Low-intensity waves
  • Yellow: Medium-intensity waves
  • Red: High-intensity, dangerous waves
